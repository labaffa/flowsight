This interactive tool is dedicated to monitoring digital spaces for conversations, news coverage and search engine interest related to FEWS NET’s dimensions of food security early warning.

The objective of this dashboard is to support food security analysts and practitioners to explore and understand how food security shocks and outcomes are represented in different platforms, focusing on digital content disseminated in FEWS NET-covered countries.

By selecting the food security domain you would like to visualize below, the map will display the latest topic anomaly score for our digital data sources. To visualize other metrics, click the button on the top right corner of the map and select the desired data streams and layers.

Hover over a country to see a tooltip and click to navigate to the country-level analyses. A full user guide is available in the top menu.
