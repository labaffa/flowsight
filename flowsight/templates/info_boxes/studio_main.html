To enable exploration of all potential combinations of metrics and analytics, users can create their own visualizations.

Once a query has been built in the "Build a query" filter bar, several functionalities are available in the Chart Studio. After all desired fields are selected, the chart can be generated by clicking the "Create" button in the center of the page.

The social listening and media monitoring content supporting the analysis will appear in the two widgets below the visualization.

The right column enables customizing some of the features of the visualization: main title and axes titles; background color; and text color. Several options for exporting the image and its background data are available by clicking the icon on the top right corner of the graph.
