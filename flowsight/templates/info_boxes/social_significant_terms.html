The word cloud displays the 50 words with the highest average TF-IDF scores, for the selected period. The "term frequency-inverse document frequency" (TF-IDF) is a common unsupervised text mining metric that identifies how important a word is to a document in a collection or corpus. In this analysis, the TF-IDF is calculated based on the collection of Telegram posts for each country, by period, in order to identify emerging topics in the conversations.
