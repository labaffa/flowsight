This timeline presents a comparative overview of topic attention, by data source (news and social media).

Based on the custom taxonomy developed for 117 topics of interest to FEWS NET, the default timeline displays the aggregated Attention metric over time for each data stream. By default, when no topic is filtered, the visualization displays an average of topic attention for all topics, for each data stream, per day.

Attention is based on topic prevalence, calculated from the presence of terms associated to a particular topic in a Telegram post or news story, normalized on the value of the total word count of that item and scaled on a range from 0 to 1.

Scale: 0 to 1
