This table presents the fluctuation of Attention and Sentiment per Domain. The first figure per metric is the aggregated domain Attention and Sentiment. To their right, the percentage change in relation to the same period prior to the selected timespan is presented. For the Attention metric, figures in green indicate a rise in the variable, whereas figures in red indicate a drop in attention. For Sentiment, green figures indicate sentiment change was positive, whereas red figures indicate the sentiment change was negative.

Scale: 0 to 1 for the metric values, 0-100% for period fluctuations.
